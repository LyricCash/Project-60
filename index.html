My favorite hobby is writing. With writing, it gives me a break away from the computer and it allows me to express my creativity. You don't have to have good handwriting or a good typing speed to write, and the only limit is your imagination. Whether it be an elaborate story, a small paragraph about a particular topic, a debate with multiple perspectives, or anything of the sorts, writing can be an enjoyable experince that you can come back to time and time again.
